Oslo Impro Festival 2024: 6th Edition

Celebrating global improv with teams and teachers from all around the world. Join us for shows and workshops every day from October 12th to 19th!

Craig Uhlir - USA

Reignite your improv skills with Craig, a seasoned artist from Chicago, ready to electrify the stage once again with his dynamic approach.

Stacey Smith - USA/Netherlands

Dive into creativity and musical improv with Stacey from Boom Chicago, inspiring with her energetic style and inclusive teaching methods.

Chris Mead - UK

Experience the art of improv with Chris from Hoopla Impro, featuring a rich background in teaching across 25 countries. Don't miss his performances with Project2 and Duologues.

Billy Kissa - Greece/Belgium

Explore bold choices in improv with Billy, a vibrant actress blending theater and stand-up comedy techniques for a powerful stage presence.

Balasree Viswanathan - India

Join Bala for a unique blend of Indian arts and improv, enriching the festival with vibrant performances and deep cultural insights.
